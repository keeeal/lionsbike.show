LIONS BIKE SHOW

Ride to Macclesfield in the Adelaide hills and enjoy a day filled with everything to do with motorcycles! Includes bike show, swap meet, live music, BBQ, bar, coffee and desserts.

Bikes of all ages, makes and nationalities are welcome! $5 entry and children under 14 are free.

Exhibitors

$10 per person entry, unlimited bikes and a free Shannons goodie bag!

Email your name and details of the bikes you will be bringing to ensure your place on the oval.

Clubs

$10 per person, unlimited bikes and a trophy for best club display!

Email details of your club, the names of the members attending, and the bikes they will be bringing to ensure your place on the oval.

Traders

Traders entry fee is $30, B.Y.O. gazebo.

Download the stall holders declaration and fill it out ready to avoid delays at the gate.
